We believe that everyone should play by the same rules. Businesses are not able to influence TripAdvisor to improve their reviews, ratings or Traveler Ranking.

We have unique processes for moderating the content submitted to TripAdvisor, as well as proprietary algorithms that calculate a business' daily rank within our Traveler Ranking. We believe that businesses who don't play by the rules should be penalized.

Businesses know that consumers rely heavily on reviews when making travel decisions.
